What is your skin type?

Descriptions:

Oily Skin

  • Produces excess sebum, making the skin look shiny or greasy.
  • It benefits from lightweight, oil-free, mattifying products.

Dry Skin

  • Lacks moisture, leading to a feeling of dryness and flakiness.
  • Benefits from hydrating and nourishing products.

Combination

  • A mix of oily and dry areas.
  • Benefits from balanced products that deliver lightweight hydration.

Normal

  • Well-balanced with no excess sebum or dryness.
  • Benefits from simple products.
